üìö The Three Core Lessons

Lesson 1

Colors & Feelings

Focus: Emotional expression through color

Inspired by: Mark Rothko üé®

Materials:

  • Thick paper (A4/A3)
  • Poster/acrylic paint
  • Brushes, sponges, or fingers

Activity: Paint large areas of color, let colors overlap

Lesson 2

Lines & Movement

Focus: Emotional release through movement

Inspired by: Abstract expressionism üí´

Materials:

  • Large paper (A3+)
  • Paint & various tools
  • Floor/table covering

Activity: Move brush freely‚Äîfast, slow, dripping, splashing

Lesson 3

My Safe Place

Focus: Sense of safety & boundaries

Inspired by: Paul Klee üè∞

Materials:

  • Large paper (A3/A4)
  • Markers or crayons
  • Pencil (optional)

Activity: Draw a boundary shape, create patterns inside

üí° Continuing at Home

After the 3 lessons: Keep the same routine, change themes as needed. Repetition is encouraged, not variety!

Themes to Explore:

  • Emotional: Calm/busy, Heavy/light
  • Self: Me today, My body, My favorite things
  • Safety: Things that make me feel safe, My quiet place
  • Relationships: Me and my family, Someone I trust
  • Change: Before/after, Something new
  • Open: Anything I want, Free drawing
üìñ Art References: Use art books, museum websites, simple image searches, or your child's own drawings!
